<section class="section" id="about">
  <h2>About the Lab</h2>
  <p>
    The <strong>Learning Acceleration & Innovation Lab (LAAIL)</strong> focuses on
    building practical, scalable solutions at the intersection of
    <strong>Education, AI, Generative AI, and Data Science</strong>.
  </p>
  <p>
    Our work spans from <em>AI-powered educational applications</em> and
    <em>assessment platforms</em> to <em>industry-aligned learning journeys</em>,
    hackathons, and research in AI for Education.
  </p>
</section>

<section class="section two-column" id="mission-vision">
  <div>
    <h2>Mission</h2>
    <p>
      To accelerate learning using intelligent systems, empower educators and learners
      with AI-first tools, and lead innovation in educational technology and Data Science.
    </p>
  </div>
  <div>
    <h2>Vision</h2>
    <p>
      A world where AI democratizes access to high-quality, personalized education
      for every learner — from K-12 to working professionals.
    </p>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section" id="projects">
  <h2>Flagship Projects</h2>
  <div class="card-grid card-grid-3">
    <div class="card project-card">
      <h3>Evalyn – AI-Powered Assignment Lab</h3>
      <p>
        A hybrid desktop + cloud tool built on <strong>nbgrader</strong> and
        <strong>LLMs (OpenVINO, Intel AI PC)</strong> for automatic coding
        assignment creation, grading, and feedback.
      </p>
      <p class="project-tags">
        <span>nbgrader</span><span>LLM</span><span>Assessment</span>
      </p>
    </div>
    <div class="card project-card">
      <h3>AI Resume Screener & Interview Agent</h3>
      <p>
        An intelligent pipeline that parses resumes, matches candidates to job
        descriptions, and generates tailored interview questions.
      </p>
      <p class="project-tags">
        <span>LLM</span><span>Recruitment</span><span>Analytics</span>
      </p>
    </div>
    <div class="card project-card">
      <h3>SmartDoc Chat – RAG for Education</h3>
      <p>
        A production-grade RAG system using <strong>LangChain</strong>,
        <strong>FastAPI</strong>, and <strong>Docker</strong> for interactive Q&amp;A
        over course documents, policies, and learning resources.
      </p>
      <p class="project-tags">
        <span>RAG</span><span>FastAPI</span><span>Docker</span>
      </p>
    </div>
    <div class="card project-card">
      <h3>AIPC Learning Labs</h3>
      <p>
        Hands-on AI labs powered by <strong>Intel AI PCs</strong> for students,
        enabling on-device GenAI experiments and classroom activities.
      </p>
      <p class="project-tags">
        <span>Intel AIPC</span><span>On-device AI</span>
      </p>
    </div>
    <div class="card project-card">
      <h3>DSI – AI for Kids</h3>
      <p>
        An initiative introducing AI, coding, and creative GenAI tools to
        <strong>students from class 6 to 10</strong> through kid-friendly labs, demos,
        and projects.
      </p>
      <p class="project-tags">
        <span>K-12</span><span>AI Literacy</span>
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
